Join the Atoms & Isotopes Mini-Course

You’ll be taken to Canvas to create a free account or sign in. After the pilot week, student accounts are cleared and the course is refreshed for the next group.

How It Works

  1. Click “Join the Atoms & Isotopes Mini-Course”.
  2. Create a free Canvas account or sign in.
  3. Enroll in the 3-session mini-course (2 hours per session).
  4. At the end of the week, we reset accounts and prep for the next cohort.
